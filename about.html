ABOUT

Organizing student's information

and making it

Globally accessible

Our goal is to take the work out of the mundane, enabling teachers and students of the prestigious B.M.S College of Engineering to utilize their time to the fullest.

Our Student portal is to be a commonly used phrase to describe the login page where students can provide a user name and password to gain access to an education organization's programs and other learning related materials. For example, a learner who has enrolled in an online certification program may use a student portal to access online course materials, such as articles, lectures and videos, hosted on the college's servers. Student portals may be used to provide information about their attendance, special events in college, course details, assignments, records and contact information.
